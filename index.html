  • Do you offer interpreter services for non-Arabic-speaking patients?

    In-Person Interpreters: We have a team of trained and certified in-person interpreters available on-site to facilitate effective communication between our medical staff and non-Arabic-speaking patients. These interpreters are fluent in multiple languages and are skilled in medical terminology.

  • What is the hospital's emergency room policy?

    Triage System: When you arrive at our ER, you will be assessed by a skilled nurse as part of our triage system. This system prioritizes patients based on the severity of their condition. The most critical cases receive immediate attention, while other patients may experience some wait time, but we strive to keep it as brief as possible.

    Insurance Information: We accept all patients, regardless of their ability to pay or their insurance status. No one will be turned away from our ER due to financial reasons.

    Visitation Guidelines: For the safety and privacy of all patients, we limit the number of visitors in the ER. Please be prepared for potential restrictions on the number of visitors allowed in the treatment area.

    Patient Rights: You have the right to informed consent, privacy, and confidentiality in the ER. Our staff will explain your treatment options and obtain your consent for any procedures.

    Medical History and Information: It's essential to provide accurate and complete medical history and information to our healthcare team, including any allergies, current medications, and pre-existing conditions. This information assists us in delivering the best care possible.

    Billing and Charges: ER services are billed separately from other hospital services. We will work with you to ensure you understand the billing process and assist in any way we can to answer your questions.

    Follow-Up Care: After receiving treatment in the ER, we may recommend follow-up care or provide instructions for continued recovery. Please follow these recommendations to ensure your well-being.
